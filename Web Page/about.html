About Me

My Journey

Hi, I am Adeeba Rafi. I started learning web design because I was curious about how websites work. At first, everything looked too hard, but once I began to understand HTML and CSS, I could not stop. I enjoy creating clean and simple designs and turning ideas into real pages. Every small project helps me learn something new. This website is one of those projects, made with love and a bit of struggle along the way. I believe learning never stops, and I am just getting started.

Where I Come From

I am from Mansehra, a peaceful place surrounded by green hills and fresh air. The water here is clear, and the fruits and vegetables taste amazing. It is a great place to live if you love nature. But to be honest, it is not perfect. The electricity goes out too often, and the internet can test your patience. Still, these small problems never stop me from learning and chasing my goals. I have learned to stay creative even when the lights go out. Maybe that is what makes life here fun and full of stories.
